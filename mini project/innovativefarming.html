INNOVATIVE FARMING

IoT in Agriculture: Internet of Things (IoT) devices enable real-time monitoring and management of agricultural processes through sensors and automation systems.

Geographic Information System: Geographic Information Systems (GIS) help in mapping and analyzing soil, crop conditions, and weather patterns to improve agricultural planning and decision-making.
AI/ML in Agriculture :Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning enhance predictive analytics for crop yield, disease detection, and optimized resource utilization in farming.
Agricultural Robotics:Agricultural robotics automate labor-intensive tasks like planting, weeding, and harvesting, increasing efficiency and productivity
Drones in Agriculture: Drones provide aerial imagery and data collection for crop monitoring, irrigation management, and precision agriculture practices.
Agricultural Biotechnology: Biotechnology involves using genetic engineering and molecular techniques to develop crops with improved traits such as pest resistance and increased yield.
